Database: ai_data_analyst_agent

Table: customer_churn

Columns:
- customer_id: Unique customer ID
- customer_name: Customer or account name
- region: Customer region such as East, West, South, or North
- subscription_plan: Customer subscription plan such as Basic, Standard, or Premium
- monthly_charges: Monthly subscription charge amount
- tenure_months: Number of months the customer has stayed with the company
- contract_type: Type of contract such as Month-to-Month, Annual, or Two-Year
- payment_method: Customer payment method
- support_tickets: Number of support tickets raised by the customer
- churn_status: Indicates whether the customer churned. Values are Yes or No
- churn_reason: Reason why the customer left or cancelled the service. Use this column when the question asks for top churn reasons, reasons customers churn, why customers leave, or why customers cancel.

Important:
- The table contains one row per customer.
- churn_status = 'Yes' means the customer churned.
- churn_status = 'No' means the customer is still active.
- churn_reason is only meaningful for customers where churn_status = 'Yes'.
- monthly_charges, tenure_months, and support_tickets are numeric columns.
