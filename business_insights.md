# Customer Churn Analysis - Business Insights

## 1. Objective

The objective of this analysis is to understand customer churn patterns using customer subscription data. The analysis focuses on overall churn rate, churn by region, churn by subscription plan, and top churn reasons.

## 2. Dataset Overview

The dataset contains customer-level subscription information, including region, subscription plan, monthly charges, tenure, contract type, payment method, support tickets, churn status, and churn reason.

## 3. Key Analysis Questions

1. What is the overall churn rate?
2. Which region has the highest churn rate?
3. Which subscription plan has the highest churn rate?
4. What are the top reasons customers churn?

## 4. Key Findings

### Overall Churn

The churn rate output shows the percentage of customers who discontinued service compared to the total customer base.

### Churn by Region

The churn by region output helps identify which geographic region has the highest customer churn risk.

### Churn by Subscription Plan

The churn by subscription plan output helps identify whether Basic, Standard, or Premium customers are more likely to churn.

### Top Churn Reasons

The churn reason output helps identify common drivers behind customer churn, such as high price, poor support, low usage, or competitor offers.

## 5. Business Recommendations

1. Focus retention campaigns on regions with higher churn rates.
2. Review pricing strategy if high price is one of the top churn reasons.
3. Improve customer support experience if poor support is a frequent churn reason.
4. Create engagement campaigns for customers with low usage.
5. Monitor Basic plan customers more closely if they show higher churn.
